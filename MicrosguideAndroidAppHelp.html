A Field Guide to the Smaller Moths of Great Britain and Ireland

Android App Help

The App provides access to the species accounts from A Field Guide to the Smaller Moths of Great Britain and Ireland. The book's pdf should be referred to for other information and explanations of the terms used.

Use the filters to refine the species list by stage or month using the dropdown fields, or click on the clover-leaf icon to filter by host plant.

The Text search field dynamically looks within the species code, species name and notes parts of the text of each species account. The number of species with the current filters applied is shown.

Example: if a blotch mine is found on hawthorn in September then select Mine in the stage field, 9 in the month, click the leaf icon, type hawthorn and tap on the plant name and finally type blotch into the Text search field.

It is important to note that polyphagous species will not be shown by this search. A further search using one of the plant species starting 'Polyphagous ..' as a filter should be undertaken.

Double-tap on an account to open the ukmoths.org.uk web page for the species. These pages can have links to leafmines.co.uk.

If you have read and want to exclude an account from your deliberation then swipe an item to the left to collapse it. Swipe to the right to show its details (periods, notes) again.

Once the filters are set they are hidden when the account list is scrolled up to give more space for the species accounts. Scroll back to the top or tap the bar with the down-arrow to show them again.
