Privacy Policy for MyRecords

1. Introduction

MyRecords (“the App”) is developed to help users view their current GPS location and record information associated with that location. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information when you use it.

By using MyRecords, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Location Data

The App accesses your device’s GPS location to display your current position as an OS grid reference (within the UK) or as latitude and longitude coordinates (outside the UK).

When you first use the App, Android will ask for your permission to allow MyRecords to access your device’s location. You can choose whether to grant or deny this permission. The App cannot determine your position or save a location-based record without this access.

Location data is processed only on your device and is never transmitted or shared with the developer or any third parties.

b. User-Entered Information

You can enter your own notes or information as part of each record. These records are stored locally in daily CSV files in your device’s Documents folder. You have full control over these files and can open, share, or delete them as you wish. No record content is uploaded or shared externally.

c. Analytics Data (User-Consented)

MyRecords uses Google Analytics for Firebase to collect anonymous usage data, such as app version, crash reports, and device type. This analytics feature is optional:

  • When you first launch the app, you are asked whether you agree to share anonymous usage data.
  • If you consent, anonymized data will be sent to Google Analytics.
  • If you do not consent, or later withdraw consent, no analytics data will be collected or transmitted.
  • You can change your consent at any time from the Help section of the App.

Analytics data never includes your location or record information and uses only a randomly generated device identity (GUID).

3. How We Use the Information

  • Display your current location within the app.
  • Save and manage your location-based records locally.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs through anonymous, consented analytics.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or sell any personal, location, or record data. The only third-party service is Google Analytics for Firebase, which receives anonymous usage data only if you have consented.

5. Storage and Security

All user records are stored locally on your device. You have complete control over your data and may delete it at any time. The developer does not access or store any user content externally.

6. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Location (GPS): Required to determine and display your current position. You will be asked by Android to grant this permission before the App can access your location.
  • Storage (read/write): Used to save your CSV records in your Documents folder.

These permissions are used solely for the functionality described above.

7. Children’s Privacy

MyRecords is not intended for children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect any personal information from them.

8. Your Privacy Choices

  • View, open, and delete your locally stored CSV records.
  • Withdraw or grant consent for anonymous analytics at any time in the Help section.
  • Manage or revoke location permission at any time through your device’s Settings.
  • Uninstalling the App does not delete the stored record data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Effective date” above. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised policy.
